About Our Clinic

Our Clinical Philosophy & Team

We believe that a dental appointment should be positive, empowering, and completely comfortable. Learn what makes our clinic different.

Redefining What a Dental Visit Can Be

Founded in Mumbai, Smile Dental Clinic was created to remove the stress and clinical coldness associated with traditional dental treatments. We reimagined the entire workflow, focusing heavily on modern biomimetic dentistry (preserving as much natural tooth structure as possible) and gentle care.

We operate with absolute transparency. From showing you 3D maps of your teeth during consultations to detailing every itemized cost beforehand, we build long-term relationships based on real trust.

Advanced Clinic Technology

Advanced Dental Equipment

We believe that better technology leads to cleaner diagnostics, less discomfort, and faster recovery times.

  • 3D Intraoral Scanner: No more messy goop. We capture highly precise 3D digital impressions of your teeth in seconds.
  • Waterlase Dental Laser: Painlessly treat gums and cavities with gentle laser light energy, reducing the need for drill vibrations.
  • Digital Low-Radiation X-rays: Instant high-res diagnostics with 90% less radiation exposure than traditional film formats.
