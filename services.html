Sedation Options for Dental Anxiety

We understand that dental anxiety is real. Smile Dental Clinic offers multiple levels of sedation to ensure your comfort throughout any procedure.

Nitrous Oxide

"Laughing gas" that provides a floating feeling of relaxation. Wears off quickly so you can drive home.

Oral Sedation

A small, prescribed tablet taken prior to appointment that creates deep relaxation and sleepiness.

IV Sedation

Deep twilight sleep managed by an anesthesiologist. Recommended for complex surgeries or high anxiety.
